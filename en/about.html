About us

1. The creators

This website was designed by Sana Deiri and Constantin Hentgen, two students from ESAIP engineering school. This project was carried out over two years. All the statistics presented on the site were created at from an official database from the government website data.gouv and then formatted using Microsoft Excel. Also, this site represents a technical challenge since it has been fully coded in HTML, CSS, JavaScript and PHP, no help service for the creation of site has not been used.

2. Our objectives

We have audience objectives, indeed, since this site is part of a school project, the latter should provide evidence of a certain success, and given the nature of our content, it will be had to reach as many people as possible. We are thus observing the evolution of the number of connections and also the number of IPs already connected.

Our deep desire is to stimulate debates and reflections on the theme of the democracy. We want as many people as possible to ask themselves about their place in the large social group. that our respective nations and, of course, humanity represent. It is obviously a choice that we let's assume.
